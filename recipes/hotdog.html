Hotdog

Description

An American classic. Easy to make, easy to enjoy. Great for the big game barbecue or a midnight snack.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Sausages
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Relish

Steps

  1. Grill or fry sausages until well cooked.
  2. Lightly heat buns on pan or in toaster.
  3. Add sausage to bun and add ketchup, mustard, and relish. Enjoy!
