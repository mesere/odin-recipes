Pasta

Description

Mario's favorite dish. Great for family dinners or romantic dates.

Ingredients

  • Penne Rigate
  • Tomato sauce
  • Minced beef
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Bring lightly salted water to boil in a pot
  2. Once boiling add desired amount of penne rigate and wait 8 minutes
  3. While the pasta is cooking heat up a pan and cooked minced beef in it
  4. Once minced beef is nearly cooked add tomato sauce and stir
  5. Strain pasta and put back into pot with a little bit of olive oil
  6. Mix pasta with minced beef and sauce
  7. Sprinkle a little bit of cheese and enjoy!
