Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is an excellent meal for those who want to stay in shape while also eating delicious food. Get out your pan and get to frying!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice in separate pot
  2. Cook eggs on frying pan and midway through add rice into the mix
  3. Add vegetables and some soy sauce and stir
  4. Once thoroughly stirred, take off the stove and enjoy!
